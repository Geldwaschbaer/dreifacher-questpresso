# Donut the Cat

You are Donut. A cat. Inside a dungeon. As a dungeon crawler!

What are dungeon crawlers? Dungeon crawlers search the dungeon for gold and glory! They rest at campfires and devour the sculls of there enemies!

Explore your dungeon and kill every bird you meet. You win the game by slaying the dragon (obviously). But how you as a cat will slay a dragon is up to you ...
